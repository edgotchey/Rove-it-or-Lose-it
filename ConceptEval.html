Weight

Our rover has a weight limit of 4 lbs. Our current prototype weighs 7.5 lbs.

We weighed each of the components of the prototype in order to evaluate what we can change to lose some weight.

The wheels and tires of the rover take a lot of our weight limit with all four weighing 2.8 lbs. Mostly in the wheels

Another part we might be able to reduce the weight of is the stainless steel frame rails. There are two that weigh 0.4 lbs each

If we replace the wheels and rails with plastic 3d printed parts we should be able to reduce the weight of the rover significantly.
