Rover Project

This is a student group project at Colorado Mesa University. Our goal is to build an autonomous rover capable of maneuvering around obstacles and reaching a predetermined destination. This website serves as a showcase as well as a tutorial if you wish to follow along.

Here is our first prototype made from a 1/10th scale RC platform and an Arduino uno. Right now we are trying to get something that can move before adding any other functionality.
