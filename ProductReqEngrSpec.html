Rove it or Lose it!

Ranked Product Requirements

In order the rover must be able to:

1.Navigate to specified gps coordinates through rough, uneven, and sandy terrain

2.Capable of obstacle avoidance and RF communication

3.Deliver a cubic payload of 1.5 inches cubed

4.Withstand an 18 inch vertical drop test with no loss of functionality

5.Rover mission specific speed and accuracy requirements

Initial Engineering Specifications

-Transmit and interpret GPS encoded data using arduino and RF within 10 seconds

-Drive to specified location with an accuracy of +/- 10% in either direction

-Achieve a speed of 1.5ft/s to be able to complete 30 feet in 30 seconds after processing time

-Actively avoid obstacles while driving 99.7% of the time the rover encounters an obstacle

-Navigate via GPS with a heading accuracy of +/- 10 degrees
