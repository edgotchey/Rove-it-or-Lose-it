Mission 2

-This mission is about obstacle avoidance, first we will drive in an enclosed space around 6x6 feet. The rover cannot contact any walls or obstacles for 60 seconds.

-The next part of the mission involves driving forward to a waypoint 30 feet directly infront of the rover. There will be a single obstacle in the way which the rover must navigate around and stop within 5ft of the marked waypoint in under 120 seconds.
