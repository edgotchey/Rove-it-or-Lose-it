Rove it or Lose it!

Mission 1

-Drive in a 10 foot square pattern, stopping at the original location and placing a marker. The marker should land within 3 feet of the rover's original location.

The mission needs to be completed in under 60 seconds.

-Seperately we need to drive straight for 15-30 feet, specified on mission day and place a marker. To complete this mission the rover needs to use the RF transmitter beacon to read the specific intended distance. The marker needs to be within +/- 10% of the specified drive distance and should be completed in less than 30 seconds.

We possibly could use a modified version of the prototype MK1 for our first mission
